DotEasy Sucks

For my first post on my brand new blog, I would just like to announce that it took DotEasy a week (that’s right, A WEEK) to give me my auth code and release my domain for transfer to GoDaddy after I originally requested it.

First they wanted to know why I wanted to switch to another registrar. My answer should have been, “because you’re the type of company that asks such questions, requiring me to write another email to get rid of your crappy service, once and for all,” but I wimped out and just said that I was happier with the service that GoDaddy and Hostgator provide me with my other domains.

because you’re the type of company that asks such questions, requiring me to write another email to get rid of your crappy service

Then, after they got around to answering my second email, 24 hours later, I got the “Are you sure you want to do this?” email, which resulted in another 5 days of waiting.

Now that the transfer has finally gone through, I’ve completely rid myself of Dot(not-so)Easy.
